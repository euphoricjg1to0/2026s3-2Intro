About The Smiler

The Smiler is a world-famous roller coaster located at Alton Towers in the UK. Opened in 2013, it is known for its intense ride and unique psychological theme.

Ride Statistics

  • Inversions: 14 (World Record)
  • Top Speed: 85 km/h
  • Length: 1,170 meters
  • Duration: 2 minutes 40 seconds
  • Opened: 2013

Ride Experience

Riders are taken through a chaotic journey filled with loops, corkscrews, and sudden drops. The ride features two lift hills, including a vertical lift that builds anticipation before plunging riders into more intense inversions.

Theme & Story

The Smiler is themed around mind control and forced happiness. The ride's central structure, known as the "Marmaliser", is designed to "correct" riders and make them smile.
